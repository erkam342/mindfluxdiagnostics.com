EEG interpretation services

We provide flexible remote interpretation coverage that can complement your existing neurology staff or fully support EEG services where in-house expertise is limited.

Neonatal & pediatric EEG

Interpretation of neonatal and pediatric EEG recordings, including seizure detection, encephalopathy assessment, and age-appropriate background analysis.

Adult & ICU continuous EEG

Routine, prolonged, and continuous EEG studies for adult patients in ward or ICU settings, with attention to non-convulsive seizures, status epilepticus, and encephalopathy patterns.

Intracranial EEG & complex cases

Support for intracranial EEG monitoring and difficult-to-localize epilepsy, including correlation with clinical semiology and presurgical planning when needed.
