How a remote EEG partnership works

We integrate with your existing EEG workflow and EMR as simply as possible. The goal is straightforward: high-quality, timely reports that your clinicians can trust.

1. Introductory discussion

  • Review your EEG volume, typical study types, and desired turnaround times.
  • Clarify coverage needs (weekday, weekend, backup, or niche subspecialty support).
  • Align on reporting format and communication preferences.

2. Technical setup

  • Secure remote access to your EEG platform (e.g., VPN, cloud-based viewer).
  • Confirm how finalized reports are transmitted back (EMR, fax, secure messaging).
  • Define stat / urgent notification pathway if needed.

3. Pilot phase

  • Start with a defined set of routine and/or cEEG studies.
  • Collect feedback from your neurology and ICU teams.
  • Adjust language, structure, and thresholds based on real-world use.

4. Ongoing coverage

  • Consistent interpretation workflows once your team is comfortable with the process.
  • Periodic review of cases and quality metrics.
  • Flexible scaling up or down depending on volume and staffing changes.
